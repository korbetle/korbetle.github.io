Why learn English?

As we know, English is a global language. Many articles write in English and many people all over the world speak English. Especialy you work in IT field.

Language skills claasify

Basicly, there four language skills: Reading,Writing,Speaking,Listening. LSRW as a short. Many people can read with assistant of a dictionary, but can’t speak out freely. Speaking is totally different to Reading. But speaking is a important skill when you want communicate with orthers directly.

How to improve SPEAKING skill?

  • First of all, you should do more listening before you can speaking.
  • master a large amount vocabulary.
  • Do more speaking exercise.

There is no shortcut for learn a language.
